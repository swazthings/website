about me

sai srinivas is an 8th grader from india, who, on one fateful rainy summer night, had the bright idea of making himself a website. while he is a native of chennai, he lives in bangalore.

his hobbies include music, sports, and stamp collecting. he also holds a 1st dan black belt in itf taekwondo, and is a decent table tennis player. he is a supporter of liverpool fc.

a self-proclaimed "god", most people who know sai would describe him as an absolute nerd.
